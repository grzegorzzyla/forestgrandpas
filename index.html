ATARI XL/XE
demoscene music
vol. 1
All tracks on this album were recorded directly through an analog signal path from a real 8-bit Atari computer with two original POKEY chips, and then analog-mastered at EPROM SOUNDS STUDIO. We do believe this effort was worth it, as it allowed us to preserve the soul of this wonderful music.
